Step By Step in a Nutshell

 "Step By Step" is an american sitcom which premiered on September 20, 1991 on the ABC Network. The show aired its first six seasons on this Network and then migrated to the CBS Network on September 97 to continue on with its Seventh Season.

Step By Step is from Bickley-Warren Productions and Miller-Boyett Productions in association with Warner Bros. Television. Executive producers are Thomas L. Miller, Robert L. Boyett, William Bickley, Michael Warren, and Ross Brown.

Step by Step has not been renewed for an eighth season and is no longer showing on CBS. Most likely the last original, prime time Step by Step has been seen. To search for syndicated reruns in your area, go to this page.

The show portrays the life of the Lambert-Foster family. It is very much like the Brady Bunch; however, none of the plots, save the very first episode, were copied from the Brady Bunch. The writers tried to modify the main storyline to fit the 90's. This page is dedicated to Step By Step and its cast, makers, and viewers.
